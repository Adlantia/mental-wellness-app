Persona: Andromeda

Profession: Digital Marketing Specialist

Lifestyle and Background: Andromeda leads a busy lifestyle balancing her career in a fast-paced startup environment with her social life and personal hobbies. She is health-conscious and has a keen interest in maintaining her mental well-being. With a background in digital marketing, Andromeda is tech-savvy and appreciates well-designed apps that offer a seamless user experience.

Technology Usage: Andromeda is an Apple user, with an iPhone 14 and Macbook Pro for work and Macbook Air for personal things. She is an early adopter and quick to try new apps, especially if they might help streamline her day-to-day activities. She prefers apps that are intuitive, aesthetically pleasing, and provide personalized experiences.

Attitudes and Behaviors: Andromeda values efficiency and effectiveness in tools she uses, especially those related to health and wellness. She is proactive about managing her mental health and seeks solutions that can be easily integrated into her daily routine. She is open to trying new technologies that promise to enhance her lifestyle and well-being.

Frustrations and Needs:

  • Frustrations: Andromeda is often frustrated by apps that are cluttered, complex, or have a steep learning curve. She dislikes tools that disrupt her daily flow or require too much time to maintain.
  • Needs: She needs a mental wellness tracker that is straightforward and quick to use, offering a daily tracking feature that fits seamlessly into her routine. The app should provide insightful feedback and encouragement without overwhelming her.

Goals with the Daily Tracking Feature: Andromeda seeks to effortlessly record her daily mental wellness activities and moods to identify patterns and triggers. She values concise, actionable insights that help her make informed decisions about her mental health. She wants the daily tracking to be a calming, reflective process that contributes positively to her day, not just another task on her to-do list.

User Story

As a user, Andromeda wants to log her daily anxiety levels to better understand triggers and manage her anxiety.

Interaction Flow

Precondition: Andromeda is logged into her account

Postcondition: Andromeda's daily anxiety levels are recorded, providing data for analysis and personalized insights

  1. Andromeda opens the mental wellness tracker app on her smartphone.
  2. The app greets her and displays the main menu, including a "Track Anxiety" button.
  3. Andromeda taps on "Track Anxiety" to access the anxiety tracking feature.
  4. The app presents a simple scale, ranging from "Calm" to "High Anxiety," for her to rate her current anxiety level.
  5. Andromeda selects her anxiety level and has the option to note any specific triggers or events that may have contributed (e.g., work stress, social interaction).
  6. After completing the exercise, Andromeda confirms her final anxiety level and submits her entry.
  7. The app acknowledges the submission and offers a quick tip or encouragement, tailored to her reported anxiety level and recent entries.
  8. Andromeda is directed back to the main menu, where she can view her anxiety trend over time or explore other wellness features.
