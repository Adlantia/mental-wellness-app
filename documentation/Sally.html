Name:

Sally Green

Tech:

Devices:

For work, Sally has a HP ProBook that is runs Windows 11. Sally does have a personal laptop at home, which is a HP 14" laptop that runs Windows 10. Her cell phone is a Samsung Galaxy S21. Preferred browser is Chrome.

Love/Hate:

Sally loves technology. Sally has been around technology her whole life and likes how there is an application and website for almost everything. Sally loves having the ability to use technology to stay organized.

Proficiency:

Sally is very comfortable with tech in general, but is not a pro and likes when websites are easy to maneuver. She prefers an easy-to-use interface where she does not have to spend much time navigating within the app.

Attitude and Needs:

Sally is a very driven and hardworking person recently dealing with a career change. She often finds herself dealing with work related stressors and struggles finding ways to balance work, personal life and self-care. She is looking for ways to deal with stress management, work-life balance and also self-reflection.

Why this Mental Wellness app?

Sally does not want to have to pay money to stay organized and on top of her mental health. She searched for similar websites and applications, and thought this would be a good fit because she is able to track multiple things at once and also journal about her feelings.

User Story:

As a new user, I want to proactively manage my mental health in a convenient way.

Use Case & Interaction Flow

Sally recently got a new job and is looking for a mental wellness app to help her manage her time and work-related stress. She needs a user-friendly interface that easily fits into her now busy schedule.

Pre-condition:

Sally has the application open.

Post-condition:

Sally now has an account in the Mental wellness app.

  1. A welcome message is displayed by the app.
  2. Sally clicks on the "Create an account" button.
  3. User is directed to "Create Account" screen.
  4. Sally clicks on "Name" input field.
  5. Mental wellness app focuses on input field.
  6. Sally types their name in "Name" input field.
  7. App displays their name in input field.
  8. Sally clicks on "Email" input field.
  9. App focuses on "Email" input field.
  10. Sally types Email into "Email" input field.
  11. App displays their email in "Email" input field.
  12. Sally clicks on "Create a Password" input field.
  13. App focuses on "Create a Password" input field.
  14. Sally types new password into "Create a Password" input field.
  15. App directs Sally to "Terms and Conditions" screen.
  16. Sally quickly scrolls through "Terms and Conditions", and clicks on "Agree"
  17. Signup is complete, App directs Sally to Profile Setup.
