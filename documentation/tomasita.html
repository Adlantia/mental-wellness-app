Persona - Tomasita

Persona

Name

Tomasita

Tech

Devices

  • Chromebook
  • Budget-friendly Android phone
  • Secondhand Samsung tablet

Love/Hate

Tomasita isn't a huge fan of technology, and definitely doesn't keep up with the latest devices.

Proficiency

Nevertheless, she is quite proficient with technology, and is usually drafted into being the family tech support.

Attitudes and Needs

Tomasita has used plenty of other tools to track things, such as Google Fit for her exercise and habit trackers for other things, but these are all separate. If she wants to find out if her mood is better when she gets more consistent exercise, she has to go back and forth between different apps and try to see a trend herself. She'd like to be able to look in one place and have these trends highlighted for her. That way, she can see at a glance what kinds of things might be influencing her mood.

User Story

As a proficient user, I want to view the trends in my mood and exercise levels, which I have been tracking for a month.

Use Case and Interaction Flow

Tomasita has been feeling a bit down the last few days after having had a great week last week. She's also been too busy to fit in her usual workouts, and she wants to see if her mood was also low last time she had to skip exercise.

Precondition: Tomasita has been making daily, or near-daily logs for a few weeks and is currently logged in to the site.

Postcondition: She has looked at a graph showing the data she's logged and has been able to draw a conclusion about factors that may be influencing her mood.

  1. Tomasita is looking at the main page of the site, which has a button for "visual display." She clicks it.
  2. The site displays a page where she can build a visual display of her data.
  3. There are buttons to choose between a calendar view or graph view. She clicks the button for a graph.
  4. The site shows a list of the categories she's been logging.
  5. She selects "mood" and "exercise."
  6. The page shows a graph plotting a line for each category.
